This product includes algorithms, reference data, and validation datasets
derived from the MoSQITo project. The following standards are implemented
based on MoSQITo's reference implementations:

- **ISO 532-1:2017** — Acoustics — Methods for calculating loudness (Zwicker method)
- **ECMA-418-2:2022** — Psychoacoustic metrics for ITT equipment (hearing model loudness & roughness)
- **DIN 45692:2009** — Measurement technique for the simulation of the sharpness sensation
- **ANSI S3.5-1997** — Methods for Calculation of the Speech Intelligibility Index
- **ECMA-74 Annex D** — Tone-to-Noise Ratio (TNR) and Prominence Ratio (PR)
- **Daniel & Weber (1997)** — Roughness psychoacoustic model
